What is Ansai Technologies?

We build software that helps institutions run better—starting with schools in Africa. Our platform handles the complex operational work so leaders can focus on what matters most.

Why start with schools?

Schools are at the heart of every community. When a school runs well, the entire community benefits. They're also complex enough to prove our approach works.

What makes your platform different?

We design for real conditions—unreliable internet, mobile payments, varied devices. Our platform works offline, syncs automatically, and integrates with M-PESA natively.

What if my internet connection is unreliable?

Your data saves locally first and syncs when connected. Nothing gets lost. You can work fully offline and everything catches up automatically.
